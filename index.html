List Your Service

Independent creators can submit and list their digital products or services on our platform. You must ensure that your content is original or that you have full rights to distribute it.

Data Privacy

We do not permanently store customer data on our servers. Order details are forwarded securely to the respective seller for fulfillment.

Direct Fulfillment

Sellers are fully responsible for delivering their product or service to the buyer within the promised timeframe.

Transparent Commission

Each transaction may include a predefined commission structure. Creator-based coupon codes help us track referrals and sales attribution.

Secure Payments

Payments are processed through verified UPI channels. After payment confirmation, orders are marked for verification and processing.

Platform Responsibility

We act as a technology facilitator. The platform connects buyers and sellers, while the seller retains full responsibility for product authenticity and delivery.
